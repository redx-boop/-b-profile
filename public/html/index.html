System Flow (At a Glance)

1. Auto Camera Capture

Browser permission triggers external USB camera. Image is captured automatically on countdown.

2. Secure Signup

We hash passwords with bcrypt, store the image securely, and generate a unique UUID per user.

3. QR Generation

A unique QR token is created server-side and saved to MySQL with the QR image path.

4. Dual Login

Login with either a fresh camera capture or QR scan — both validated on the backend.
